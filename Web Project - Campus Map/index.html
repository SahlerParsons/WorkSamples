Welcome to Kampus Kops! A fun game where you protect the University of Arizona Campus, and learn your way around as well! To play the game first hit the 'start game' button. Then click on a remedy (their point values differ - but beware you have fewer of the higher point units) then click on a location on the map to solve a crime! Once you have a unit selected you do not need to reselct it if you are happy with it. For even more fun, open up your console (cntrl + shift + i). Enjoy and have fun! The game can be found here.
